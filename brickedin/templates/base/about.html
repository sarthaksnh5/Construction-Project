About Us

Our Story: Redefining construction


We, BrickedIN, are a construction company. Starting as Anamika Associates, a proprietorship, for the past 15 years, we have been turning dreams into realities and now we are a proud private limited Company called BrickedIn pvt. ltd. Whether it is a home, a workspace or a factory, we have established our expertise in everything. Slow and steady, we grew. We learnt the market demands, new technologies, budding trends and today we take pride in stating, We are among the best construction companies in the market. We believe once a foundation is laid, it is there for generations, so we put all of our love, passion, and technology into our projects.

Our team consists of civil engineers, associate contractors and very skilled, experienced laborers. With both old and young minds, we have the perfect balance of experience and creativity. Our highly professional engineers are certified assurance of best technology and innovations.

Built with devotion, BrickedIn does not take contracts just for the sake of money because Building is our passion. Our team is full of enthusiastic Civil Engineers. Their energy and interest can be overwhelming in this business of stones and sticks. Not only a good team but BrickedIn takes care of your every need: materials, their quality check, their prices, expert advice, regular updates, just everything. A guarantee of best quality work at a competitive price is what we promise you. You are also given the assurance of best materials used. How? Because we perform a quality test on about 320+parameters. From ‘moisture content in excavation work to the brick test in masonry work,’ for each stage of construction all the standard tests are performed under the scrutiny of our very own Civil Engineer. So, no worries.

When you choose us, a symbiotic relationship buds. What we gain is your trust, a happy customer for the lifetime, and of course a little money ;) What you get is:


§   Transparency (Every transaction/activity as clear as crystal)

§   Time (On time delivery)

§   Tracking (you should be able to control and track every bit of your work execution, be it material or workforce, we have built necessary tools to assist you)

§   Technology (Use of technology in planning, executing and monitoring will increase efficiency, reduce cost and allow us to produce great results for you)

We have partnered with the best brands and professionals to ensure you get the best quality service.  Our intention to create this network is to get rid of your foot work with everything available to you at a click away.

‘We understand the value of your dream home and we promise to give you a memorable one’.

Regards,
Team  BrickedIN BuildTech Private Ltd.
